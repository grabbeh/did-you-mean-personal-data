# Did you mean personal data

Frustrated with your organisation's use of the term 'PII' when they probably mean personal data, or should take into account the broad scope of personal data?

Lack the motivation and organisational gravitas to change this?

Satisfied to delegate your role to a passive aggressive slackbot?

Look no further than this handy slackbot which will automatically reply to any mention of 'PII' with the phrase 'Did you mean personal data' and nothing more.
